# BLIP Top-Down Concept Notes

Reusable ideas preserved for later top-down expansion:

- Weapons: current foundation is Pulse Carbine, Arc Blade, and Recall Disc. Older ideas like Scatter, Repeater, Lance, Echo Arc, Rupture, Seeker, and Wave are future mutations or upgrades, not separate live weapons.
- Loot: Signal Shards, hidden Signal Caches, Scout Set pieces, portrait cards, field notes, and trophies.
- Scout secrets: WILLOW route marks, SPARK machinery, ANCHOR safe zones, ECHO route memory, ROCKET speed trials.
- Stealth lights: red scan beams, rotating Classifier reads, signal barriers, and safe cover pockets.
- Enemies: Drifter, Tagger, Diver, Warden, Pinpoint, Replicator, Jitter, Pylon, Classifier, Maze Heart, and Listening Station concepts.
- Story: CONTACT-47, the Interpretation Engine, Chagrin Falls, the Five Signal Scouts, Signal Nodes, breaches, and the final refusal of a label.
