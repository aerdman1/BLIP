# BLIP Vertical Slice Design

Status: current route-connected top-down arena foundation plus first real objective/reward differentiation pass, corrected combat collision pressure, active first-route clarity pass, and an experimental Tripo CONTACT-47 model import.

The current world has schematic-first expanded layouts across all five route regions plus a first gameplay-design/content pass that gives every named area a route, branch, secret, shortcut, reward, encounter or recovery purpose. Authored field-event markers now add scan/power rewards, weapon prototypes, Scout boons, overdrive/health pockets and ambush beats across the route. A subtle discovered-area lighting pass dims unvisited HD rooms and fades them open as CONTACT-47 reaches them. It is large enough for a focused 15-25 minute playable route now, and can become a 30-45 minute vertical slice only through manual feel review, better combat pacing, stronger rewards, secrets, objectives, and targeted layout/cover adjustments. Do not convert the route into one giant Phaser scene.

Use `MAP_SCHEMATICS.md` as the map-planning source of truth. It links generated SVG schematics for all five route arenas and should be regenerated with `node scripts/generate-map-schematics.mjs` after coordinate changes.

## Systems Status

- Playable: connected top-down route chain, single-save flow, dev region warps, three-weapon foundation, fast weapon switching, route-state persistence, named region goals, Phase Shift with restrained trailing echoes, distinct enemy roles, major reward banners/save persistence.
- Weapon feel pass: Pulse Carbine is faster with stronger charged-shot feedback, Arc Blade has a wider close-range burst and always-on parry shockwave, and Recall Disc has a consistent return trail. Important weapon pickups/equip messages now resolve from the actual weapon id, show role cards, and avoid routine giant banners.
- Camera response: camera shake is damage-only and restrained. Routine shooting, impacts, kills, rewards, scanner alerts, overdrive and traversal should use sparks, flashes, rings, sound or animation instead of shaking the player camera.
- Route hardening: area transitions require standing inside the actual charged breach for a short dwell. Broad road/exit rectangle auto-warps are intentionally removed because they could feel like surprise teleports near East Road.
- Tested: combat hitboxes/contact pressure now punish idle play, HD sprite scaling no longer shrinks physics bodies, and the combat validation suite proves every enemy archetype can move or remain intentionally rooted and can be killed by real player projectiles.
- Field-event content pass: each region now has visible scan/power markers for worthwhile optional rewards, powerups, weapon prototypes, specific Scout boons and tactical ambush hooks. These markers are exposed through AI visible perception so bots can investigate them without hidden trigger knowledge.
- AI status: AI campaigns are useful playtest evidence, not the production deploy gate. Reward motivation, first-three-route completion and full-route completion still need broader evidence.

## Guardrails

- Keep the route as separate top-down arena maps unless there is a strong technical reason to merge.
- Add only the smallest enemy/hazard roles that improve the current weapons or Phase Shift.
- Every traversal mechanic must have safe spawn points, collision, camera handling, save/load behavior, and touch/controller support.
- Command Center is the live dashboard for this plan; update `src/game/data/commandCenterData.ts` when a system moves from planned to playable.
