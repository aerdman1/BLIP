# Blipstream Puzzle

Purpose: keep Blipstream rooms simple, unique, and buildable.

## Rules

- Blipstream rooms are **abstract waveform/platforming spaces** — the inside of the signal, not a physical place.
- Do NOT render literal interiors (no barn interiors, no furniture, no rooms-of-a-building).
- Visual language: black/dark background, waveform platforms (cyan/green glowing bars), red static hazards, moving scan bars, pulse gates, node switches, signal routing lines.
- A Blipstream room must be obviously visually distinct from Miller Field at a glance.
- Puzzles must be **short and understandable** — one readable idea, solvable in 1–3 minutes, objective text guides the player.
- Completing a Blipstream node MUST affect the main Miller Field level (world-state change the player can see).
- The first Blipstream node (Node A) unlocks the crop-circle gate/door in Miller Field.

## Interaction Vocabulary

- Pulse shot activates node switches; activated nodes route a visible signal pulse along wires.
- Hazards damage and knock back, they do not instant-kill; falling out of the room respawns at room entry.
- Exit gate opens when the routing is complete; exiting returns to the overworld with the solved flag set.
